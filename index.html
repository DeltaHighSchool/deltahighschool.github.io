What is Delta?

Where

We are located in Pasco off of Rd 100 just north of the freeway. The exact address is: 5801 Broadmoor Blvd Pasco WA 99301. You can still ride the regular bus to your home highschool and from there, the delta shuttle-bus will take you to Delta. You should arrive around 8 am each morning, with breakfast served until 8:25 am.

About Us

Delta is a public school that serves all three disctricts of the tri-cities: Kennewick, Pasco, and Richland. We focus on STEM (Science, Technology, Enginnering, and Math). That does not mean we neglect English though! (we still take English for four years.) We do lots of fun hands-on projects.
