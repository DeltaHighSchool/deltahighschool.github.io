What is Delta?

Where

We are located in Pasco off of Rd 100 just north of the freeway. The exact address is: 5801 Broadmoor Blvd Pasco WA 99301. You can still ride the regular bus to your home highschool and form there, the delta shuttle-bus will take you to delta. You should arrive around 8 am each morning.

About Us

Delta is a public school that serves all three disctricts of the tri cities: Kennewick, Pasco, and Richland. we focus on STEM (Science, Technology, Enginnering, and Math). That does not mean we neglect English though! (we still take English for four years.)
