Hands-On Projects

At Delta we get to do a lot of hands-on projects. This is a cornerstone of our learning style. We also learn by trial and error. In the video to the left, we got to make bottle rockets and launch them. The video was a final result of a well designed rocket. Until we got to this point, we launched many rockets, some of them exploding mid-launch. We had to learn by trial and error, fixing what went wrong each time and hoping for a better result the next time.
